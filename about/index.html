Michael Richelle

Michael's passion for music and sound is evident to those who have worked with him. Whether at the studio, venue or soundstage, providing a comfortable space to create and perform is the first priority. From that starting point the technical aspects of capturing the music takes place.


Michael is on staff at Rittenhouse Soundworks and INDRE Recording and Productions. Formerly on staff at World Cafe Live and Live Nation.


With over 25 years of studio recording, location recording, music mixing for broadcast and live sound, Michael's work history and experiences are broad.
